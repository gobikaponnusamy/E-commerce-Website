Who we are?

The way we dress reveals our personality or the way we are. Everyone has his own way of dressing. The first impression we get about somebody is the way they dress .This reveals a lot about their personality whether the person is an introvert or an extrovert. Basically introverts dress casually to avoid any special attention and to the contrary extroverts dress to add a character to their personalities as well as to look different from the crowd. Some just want to dress basic which never goes out of fashion and some like to create fashion. For them clothes add a character and drama to their personality. The more unique we dress the more confident we are viewed. So, the way we dress speaks about us!
